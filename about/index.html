About Ghillie

Self-taught digital designer and developer from Scotland. Building YouTube thumbnails, web tools, and games since 2021.

I'm a self-taught digital designer and developer from Scotland. What started as a hobby making thumbnails turned into years of working with some of YouTube's biggest creators, and eventually into building my own tools, websites, and games.

For over 3.5 years, I've been the thumbnail designer and lead video editor for Daily Dose of Internet, a channel with over 20 million subscribers. I've created hundreds of thumbnails that have collectively reached billions of views. Beyond DDOI, I've worked with creators like Daily Dose of Pets, JustRolledIn, Mattcs, Captain Eggcellent, MxR Plays, and more.

On the development side, I'm self-taught in web technologies and have built tools like a client-side SQLite viewer, GTAHub.xyz, and a Wordle solver. I also develop Minecraft plugins under the Atlas name, and co-founded Sad Ghost Studio, a small game development company.

When I'm not designing or coding, I'm usually playing games or finding something new to build. I live for the overlap between creativity and problem-solving.
