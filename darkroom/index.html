Guide & FAQ

How to compress an image for Discord
  1. Add your images. Drag photos onto the drop zone or click to browse. You can add a whole batch at once.
  2. Pick a quality. Leave the recommended setting or nudge the slider; the preview and size update instantly.
  3. Let it fit Discord. Choose your Discord tier and keep Auto-fit on so every image is guaranteed to stay under the upload limit.
  4. Download. Grab each compressed image, or hit Download all, then upload to Discord.
Is anything uploaded to a server?

No. Every image is decoded and re-compressed locally in your browser using your own device. Nothing is sent anywhere, so it is fully private and even works offline once the page has loaded.

Why does WebP look so good at such a tiny size?

WebP is a modern image format that keeps far more detail per kilobyte than the old JPEG format. At around quality 79 most photos are visually identical to the original while shrinking to a small fraction of the size.

What are Discord's upload limits?

Free accounts can upload files up to 10 MB, Nitro Basic up to 50 MB, and Nitro up to 500 MB. Pick your tier and this tool keeps every image under that limit automatically.

Does Discord show WebP, and what about transparency?

Discord displays WebP images inline just like JPG or PNG. WebP and PNG keep transparency; JPEG has no alpha channel, so transparent areas are filled with white when you export as JPEG.

Guide & FAQ

How to make a seamless Instagram carousel
  1. Add a wide photo. Drag in one wide or panoramic image. The wider it is, the more slides it fills cleanly.
  2. Choose slides and shape. Pick how many slides you want and whether they are square or portrait.
  3. Frame it. Zoom and reposition so the panorama sits exactly where you want across every slide. The swipe preview shows how it scrolls.
  4. Download and post. Hit Download all for a numbered ZIP, then upload the slides to Instagram in order for a seamless swipe.
How does a seamless carousel actually work?

Your image is sliced into equal vertical panels sized for Instagram. When you post the panels as a carousel and swipe, the cut edges line up exactly, so the eye reads the separate slides as one continuous panoramic image.

Square or portrait slides?

Square 1:1 (1080×1080) is the classic carousel look. Portrait 4:5 (1080×1350) is taller, fills more of the feed, and usually looks more dramatic for a panorama. Both are fully supported by Instagram carousels.

How do I keep the slides in the right order?

The ZIP names every slide with a number, so slide-01 is first. Add them to your Instagram post in that order and the swipe stays seamless.

Is anything uploaded to a server?

No. Everything is sliced and exported in your browser on your own device. Your photo is never sent anywhere, so it stays completely private.
